Precompiled executables:
 windows:  bin/slotti.exe (compiled with vs2017 on w10x64).
 linux:    bin/slotti     (compiled with g++ (Ubuntu 7.3.0-27ubuntu1~18.04) 7.3.0)


Compiling:
 Download Zillalib from https://github.com/schellingb/ZillaLib
 Extract it and put it like this:
 
 somedir/
   slotti/		(this game)
   Zillalib		(zillalib from github)
   
 Open .sln (visual studio) or .cbp (codeblocks) and compile.
 Or use make (check _LINK.sh).
